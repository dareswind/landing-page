Top Attractions Worth Visiting

Bantayog

The first monument built to honor José Rizal, completed on December 30, 1898. Its three-tiered stone design symbolizes Rizal's enduring legacy.

Calaguas Island

A pristine getaway, known for its white sand beaches, clear waters, and tranquil natural charm. Perfect for camping and swimming!

Mananap Falls

A picturesque 60-foot waterfall surrounded by lush forests, perfect for swimming and nature adventures.

Vinzon's Church

Built in 1611, is the oldest church in Camarines Norte, showcasing Baroque architecture and rich history.
